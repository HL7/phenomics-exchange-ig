Resource Profile: Individual

Official URL: https://github.com/phenopackets/core-ig/StructureDefinition/Individual Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2021-05-28 Computable Name: Individual

Copyright/Legal: Global Alliance for Genomics and Health

The subject of the Phenopacket is represented by an Individual element. This element intends to represent an individual human or other organism.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile:
  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Patient/Proband1, Patient/example-patient
