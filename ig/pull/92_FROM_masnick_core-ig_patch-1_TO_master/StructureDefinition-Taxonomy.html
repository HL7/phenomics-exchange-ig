Extension: Taxonomy

Official URL: https://github.com/phenopackets/core-ig/StructureDefinition/Taxonomy Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2020-02-14 Computable Name: Taxonomy

Copyright/Legal: Global Alliance for Genomics and Health

Corresponds to taxonomy (GA4GH). For resources where there may be more than one organism being studied it is advisable to indicate the taxonomic identifier of that organism, to its most specific level.
